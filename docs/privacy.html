Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Wanci ("we", "our", "the app") is a productivity and time management app.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address (via Apple Sign In). Used solely for authentication.
  • Usage data: Focus sessions, expense records, to-do items, and receipt data you create within the app. Stored in our cloud database (Supabase) and synced across your devices.
  • Device data: Device orientation and light sensor data for the skeuomorphic rendering engine. This data is processed locally and never transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and sync your productivity data across devices
  • To manage your subscription status (via RevenueCat)
  • To authenticate your account

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management. Privacy Policy
  • Apple: Sign In with Apple. Privacy Policy

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Wanci is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
